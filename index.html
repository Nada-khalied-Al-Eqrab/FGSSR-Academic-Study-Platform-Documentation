Web Application FGSSR Academic Study Platform

An integrated, web-based Academic Study Platform and Learning Management System (LMS)
custom-built for the Faculty of Graduate Studies for Statistical Research (FGSSR), Cairo University.
Aligned with Egypt’s Vision 2030 for Digital Transformation in Higher Education, this platform resolves
the inefficiencies of fragmented university services by establishing a centralized, secure, and collaborative ecosystem
for students, faculty members, and system administrators.
